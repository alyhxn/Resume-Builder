# 📄 Resume Builder

A **free, open-source resume builder** that feels like premium software. Built with modern web technologies, featuring AI-powered content enhancement, multiple templates, and professional export options.

> Tired of clunky resume builders with paywalls and mediocre quality? This is the alternative you've been looking for.

## ✨ Features

- **Multiple Professional Templates** – Choose from carefully crafted resume designs
- **Real-time Preview** – See your changes instantly as you type
- **AI-Powered Enhancement** – Leverage AI to improve your resume content (2 free uses per 24 hours)
- **Customizable Colors & Themes** – Personalize your resume's look and feel
- **PDF Export** – Download your resume as a high-quality PDF with a single click
- **Local Storage** – Your resume data is saved automatically (no account required)
- **Fully Responsive** – Build your resume on any device
- **No Ads, No Paywalls, No Tracking** – Completely free and privacy-focused

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework**: [Next.js 16](https://nextjs.org) – React framework for production
- **Language**: [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org) – Type-safe JavaScript
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS 4](https://tailwindcss.com) – Utility-first CSS framework
- **AI Integration**: [Google Generative AI](https://ai.google.dev) – Intelligent content enhancement
- **Icons**: [React Icons](https://react-icons.github.io/react-icons) – Beautiful icon library
- **Storage**: Browser LocalStorage – No backend required

## 📖 How to Use

1. **Enter Your Information** – Fill in your personal details, experience, education, and skills
2. **Choose a Template** – Pick from multiple professional resume designs
3. **Customize Your Style** – Select colors and themes that match your personality
4. **Use AI Enhancement** (Optional) – Get AI suggestions to improve your resume content
5. **Download as PDF** – Export your resume and you're done!

## 🤖 AI-Powered Enhancement
